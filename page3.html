Covid Safety Protocol

  1. All employees will be required to wear masks and wash their hands throughout their shift.
  2. All employees will be required to get monthly covid testing.
  3. Rigorous cleaning standards will be held to combat the spread of covid.
  4. Social distancing rules will be mantained whenever possible.

Food Safety Protocol

  1. All equipment and surfaces will be cleaned and sanitized regularly.
  2. All food ingredients will be fresh and refrigerated appropriately.
  3. All employees will be required to wear gloves during their shift.
